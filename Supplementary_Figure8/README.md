# Supplementary Figure 8

This folder contains cross-validation, pathway enrichment, and gene set enrichment analyses supporting epithelial plasticity programs during lung development and alveolar deficiency.

---

## Supplementary Figure 8A – Cross-Validation of Plasticity Genes

Cross-validation of epithelial plasticity-associated genes adapted from He et al. across developmental stages and PT-BPD samples.

## Supplementary Figure 8B – Cross-Validation of Plasticity Genes in Epithelial Tip Cells

Cross-validation of epithelial tip-cell plasticity signatures adapted from He et al. and evaluated across developmental epithelial populations.

## Supplementary Figure 8C – Functional Enrichment Analysis of Core Upregulated Genes

Gene Ontology enrichment analysis of core upregulated genes identified in impaired alveoli.

## Supplementary Figure 8D – GSEA Enrichment Across Epithelial Populations

Gene Set Enrichment Analysis (GSEA) comparing epithelial populations across developmental and transitional states.

## Supplementary Figure 8E – Developmental Plasticity and Senescence Scores

Comparison of plasticity and senescence enrichment scores across developmental stages.

## Supplementary Figure 8F – Proposed Model of Developmental Plasticity and Senescence

Schematic summarizing the relationship between developmental plasticity programs, senescence-associated signaling, and epithelial fate transitions.
