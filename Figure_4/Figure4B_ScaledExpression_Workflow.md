### Panel B
Scaled expression values of selected growth factor and signaling-associated genes were extracted from the integrated pediatric lung snRNA-seq dataset.

Workflow:
1. Genes of interest were selected based on developmental signaling and growth factor pathways.
2. Scaled expression values were extracted from the integrated Seurat object.
3. Average expression values were summarized across developmental stages.
4. Results were visualized as scaled expression plots.
