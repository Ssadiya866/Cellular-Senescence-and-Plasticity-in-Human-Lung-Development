# Figure 9 – Senescence Marker Validation in Irradiated Pediatric Lung PCLS

This figure presents spatial validation of senescence-associated markers in irradiated pediatric precision-cut lung slices (PCLS) using multiplex Phenocycler/CODEX imaging.

---

## Figure 9A – p21 Spatial Validation

Multiplex Phenocycler/CODEX imaging of control and irradiated pediatric PCLS.

### Workflow

- Pediatric lung PCLS were exposed to irradiation.
- Tissue sections were stained using a multiplex antibody panel.
- Images were acquired using the Akoya Phenocycler/CODEX platform.
- Representative regions were selected to visualize p21 localization across cellular compartments.

### Software

- Akoya Phenocycler/CODEX platform
- GraphPad Prism

---

## Figure 9B – Quantification of p21-Positive Cells

Cell-type-specific quantification of p21-positive cells in control and irradiated PCLS.

### Workflow

- p21-positive cells were identified from multiplex imaging datasets.
- Cell populations were classified according to marker expression profiles.
- Percentages and proportions of positive cells were calculated across experimental groups.
- Results were visualized using GraphPad Prism.

### Software

- Akoya Phenocycler/CODEX platform
- GraphPad Prism
